Prenatals

~ During prenatal visit you will meet with Dr. Jana Dehovitz, the owner of Brooklyn Heights Pediatrics.

~Dr. Dehovitz will answer your questions and provide you with information that you will need for smooth transitioning during the postpartum period and beyond. Prenatal appointments are scheduled based on availability. Expecting parents, family members, adopting parents and parents with surrogate mothers are welcome.


                         Meet The Doctor

Meet the doctor

~ Transferring families are welcome to meet the doctor before you decide if you become our patient. We will cater the meeting based to your needs and the needs of your child/children.

~ To ensure the best of care we ask for your child's past medical records. Immunizations and laboratory results at minimum, the more data you can provide (i.e. growth chart, medications, reports from evaluations and specialists etc.) the more accurate information we will have.
