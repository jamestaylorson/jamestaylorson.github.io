Reaching Provider

~ In case of an emergency, please call 911. The EMS will take you to the closest Emergency Room.


During regular business hours, please call 718-237-2122 and the receptionist will forward your call to the appropriate recipient (provider/biller/office manager/medical assistant). Your message will be answered in the order it was received.

After regular business hours, please call 718-237-2122. You will be instructed in how to reach a provider on call.
